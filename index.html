About Me

Learn a little more about me and the work that I do

I'm Katie, and I'm a Web Developer.

I'm a natural problem solver who isn't scared of a challenge and am a firm believer that being able to laugh at yourself makes any project possible.

I'm a "big picture"​ thinker who is able to see problems before they happen, leading to balanced, complete, responsive, projects in web development and digital outreach. Web development is my specialty, but working with people for people is what I do best. I believe in working towards greater digital access for all, the passion and dedication of small teams, the unlimited potential of technology, and the power of a good cup of coffee.

When I'm not working, you can find me on Reddit, cooking, playing games (WoW, Super Smash Brothers, Bioshock et al.), and reading, usually in that order.

I'm a recent HackerYou Grad and have a B.A from the University of Toronto and am currently based out of Toronto.
